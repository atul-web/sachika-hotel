Welcome to Sachika Hotels : Our Rooms







Business Room

Experience spacious comfort in our Business Rooms at Sachika Hotels. Designed for corporate travelers, these 280 to 300 sq. ft. rooms combine productivity and relaxation. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi, and enjoy coffee/tea facilities for a quick pick-me-up. Work seamlessly at the roomy desk, unwind in the armchair, and rest well on the queen bed. Essential amenities include a rain shower, hairdryer, and climate control. Elevate your business stay with us.

Suite Room

Experience ultimate luxury and breathtaking mountain views in our expansive Suite Room at Sachika Hotels. Spanning 460 sq. ft., this space offers privacy with a separate sleeping area. Enjoy serene mountain vistas, complimentary Wi-Fi, and tea/coffee facilities. Stay polished with an iron, hairdryer, and rain shower. Indulge in the living area with TV mirror casting, an armchair, and magnifying mirror. Set your room's climate and work at the desk. Luxuriate in the spacious bathroom's rain shower. Cozy bathrobes and slippers add comfort. Elevate your stay with us and relish both relaxation and elegance amidst stunning surroundings.

Studio Room

Discover comfort and ease in our spacious Studio Rooms at Sachika Hotels. With 295 sq. ft. of space, unwind and take in stunning mountain views. Enjoy the queen-sized bed and fully air-conditioned environment for a pleasant stay. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi, and savor hot beverages with the in-room tea/coffee facility. These rooms boast a separate kitchen area with modern amenities, perfect for family or extended stays. Embrace cooking at your pace. Experience relaxation and a homely atmosphere in our Studio Rooms, designed for your comfort and peace of mind. Enjoy tranquility amid breathtaking vistas and make yourself at home during your stay.

Premium Room

Experience the blend of comfort and elegance in our spacious Premium Rooms at Sachika Hotels. With 300 sq. ft., unwind while enjoying mountain views. Delight in the panorama from the bay window sitting area. Relax in the queen-sized and twin beds, surrounded by serene beauty. Thoughtful amenities include an armchair, ironing facilities on request, hairdryer, magnifying mirror, and rain shower. Individual climate control ensures your comfort. Designed for simplicity and elegance, our Premium Rooms offer a tranquil escape. Embrace serenity and rejuvenate amidst the peaceful ambiance, away from the daily rush. Perfect for solo or group travel, enjoy a comfortable stay tailored to you.
