About Us

Welcome to Sachika Hotels, where we believe in providing a hospitality experience tailored to your needs. With our exquisite rooms and suites, delightful restaurant, cozy coffee shop, and world-class amenities, we strive to ensure your stay with us is nothing short of exceptional.

Located on NH-31 in North Guwahati, Assam, Sachika Hotels is renowned for being a deluxe hotel with a wide range of facilities and amenities. Conveniently situated near the Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport (GAU), our hotel offers easy accessibility for travelers. While you're here, don't miss the opportunity to explore the ancient Hindu pilgrimage sites like the Kamakhya Temple. Business travelers will appreciate our close proximity to local industrial parks, universities, and technology companies, making us an ideal choice for those combining work and leisure.

Our commitment is to provide every guest with a luxurious hotel experience in Guwahati. Our rooms and suites are complemented by top-notch services such as room service, reliable medical services, backup generator facilities, and efficient laundry services. At Sachika Hotels, we understand the importance of modern amenities and strive to exceed your expectations
